About JKUSA

JKUSA (Jomo Kenyatta University Students Association) is the official student governance body at JKUAT, committed to representing student interests, advocating for their rights, and fostering leadership development.

We believe in empowering students through transparent elections, policy advocacy, and inclusive representation. Join us in shaping the future of student leadership!
